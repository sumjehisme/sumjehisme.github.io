    1 Cleansing your face

    ⚝ Cleansing is the essential step before doing your beautiful makeup! By using a foam making net, I create bubbles with the cleansing milk, in order to clean my pores thoroughly.

    You can easily get one if these foam making bags from Amazon, Taobao, Walmart and many other online stores.

    SANA Soy Milk Face Cleansing Foam helps clean my pores. It also gives an instant brightening effect!

    ⚝ Gently rub your face with the foam, especially on those spots that usually get oily!

    ⚝ After briefly 30 seconds, rinse your face with lukewarm tap water.

    Remember: hot water is detrimental to your skin barrier, so you must not wash your face with it, even in winter!

    2 Face care routine

    ⚝ This step is very important, if you don’t want cakey makeups like this:

    ⚝ This step needs to be done at least 10 minutes prior to your makeup, to let your face absorb the moisture.

    ⚝ Here’s my usual routine: Vitamin C Hydration Water ➨ Hada Labo Gokujyun Toner ➨ Tea tree oil ➨ Vaseline ➨ 10 minutes break (i usually eat breakfast during the break lol☺) ➨ Sunscreen

      2.1 Lip moisturising

      ⚝ Put vaseline on your lips as a lip balm. It keeps your lips from cracks for the entire school day!

      Even if you’re not putting on any makeup, this step is still A MUST.

      2.2 Acne treatment

      ⚝ I also use tea tree oil to deal with my pimples. Before covering them up with concealer, make sure to apply the oil onto your acne first.

      Recommendation: tea tree oil of THE BODY SHOP

      2.3 Sunscreen

      ⚝ Besides blocking your skin from UV, sunscreen acts as a separation layer between your skin and the makeup.

      Even if you’re not putting on any makeup, this step is also A MUST!

    3 Natural “No-makeup” Makeup

    ⚝ Finally, here's the spicy part of your morning routine – makeup!

    ⚝ However, as we're doing this for a school day, we'll definitely try to maintain as natural as possible, reaching the“no-makeup”effect.

      The Outline - "B4"

      1. Base
      2. Blush
      3. Bags under eyes ("Aegyo-sal")
      4. Baby pink lips

      3.1 Base

      ⚝ Choose a long-wear foundation that is close to your own skin tone. That's right, don't go for colours that are way too pale, you might end up having a huge colour contrast between your face and your neck!

      ⚝ To find and purchase the perfect foundation for your skin tone, I recommend NARS Shade Finder for you. Truly a life saver!

      Apply the foundation as one thin layer each time, to avoid getting a cakey finish. Then, use a damp beauty blender to spread it all over your face.

      Song Hye-gyo is definitely top-tier pretty, but the colour of her neck differs from that of her face...can be really awkward tbh.
